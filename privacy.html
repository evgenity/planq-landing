Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2025

Data Collection

PLANQ uses your device's proximity sensor to detect and count push-ups. This sensor only detects distance changes and doesn't collect any personal or identifiable information. All processing happens instantly on your device.

Data Storage

Your workout data, including push-up counts and goals, is stored locally on your device. You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app settings.

Sensor Usage

The proximity sensor is only active during workout sessions and requires no special permissions beyond standard motion and fitness tracking. The sensor data is processed in real-time and is not stored or transmitted.

Analytics

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app's performance and features. This data cannot be traced back to individual users and does not include any personal information.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or data practices, please contact us at privacy@planq.app
