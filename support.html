Quick Start

Place your phone face down on the ground during push-ups. The proximity sensor will automatically count each rep as you lower your chest toward the phone.

Common Questions

How accurate is the counter?

The proximity sensor provides highly accurate counting when the phone is properly positioned and you maintain good form during push-ups.

Does it work on all surfaces?

Yes, PLANQ works on any flat surface. Just ensure your phone is stable and won't slide during your workout.

What if I need to pause my workout?

Simply pick up your phone to pause. Place it back down in position to continue your session.
